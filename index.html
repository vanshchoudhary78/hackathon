Our Equipment

Rowing Machine

A rowing machine provides a full-body, low-impact workout that improves cardiovascular fitness, builds strength, and burns calories.

Dumbbells

Dumbbells are versatile tools for strength training, helping build muscle, improve balance, and enhance functional fitness.

Barbells

Barbells are essential for strength training, enabling heavy lifting to build muscle, increase power, and improve overall strength.

Weight Plates

Weight plates are used to adjust resistance in strength training, enhancing exercises like squats, bench presses, and deadlifts.

Kettlebells

Kettlebells are versatile tools for strength, endurance, and functional fitness, enabling dynamic exercises like swings, squats, and presses.

Bench

Benches provide support and versatility for strength training exercises like presses, rows, and step-ups, enhancing workout effectiveness.
